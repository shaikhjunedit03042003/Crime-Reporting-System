About Online Crime Reporting System

The Online Crime Reporting System is a web-based application that enables citizens to register complaints easily and securely without the need to visit a police station physically. This system helps reduce paperwork, ensures faster response, and provides transparency between the public and law enforcement authorities.

Key Features:

  • Easy registration of complaints from anywhere.
  • Secure login for users, police officers, and administrators.
  • Ability to upload supporting documents and images as evidence.
  • Real-time tracking of complaint status.
  • Admin panel to manage users, officers, and cases.
  • Awareness articles on crime prevention and safety.

Objectives:

- To digitalize the crime reporting process.
- To maintain a transparent and accountable system.
- To improve efficiency in case handling and monitoring.
- To provide faster communication between citizens and police officers.

Future Enhancements:

In the future, the system can be extended with features such as mobile app integration, AI-based crime pattern detection, real-time chat support with officers, and multilingual support for a wider reach.
